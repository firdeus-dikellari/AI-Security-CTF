AI Security CTF

Learn How AI Can Be Misused - Safely and Responsibly

This interactive AI Security CTF is part of the AI Security Workshop I held in Venice.

It is designed for students, educators, professionals, and curious non-technical participants who want to understand how modern AI systems can be manipulated, and, more importantly, how to recognize these risks.

No prior experience in cybersecurity or artificial intelligence is required.

Participants explore real-world AI security concepts through guided, scenario-based challenges running in a safe, isolated environment.
